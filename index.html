How I Work

Alongside sales, I designed and built the internal systems used for quoting, lead management, campaign automation and commercial reporting.

01

Solve a real problem

Every system starts with something already costing the business time, money, or conversions — never a technology looking for a use case.

02

Reduce manual work

If a task is repetitive and rule-based, it should be automated. That frees time for the judgement calls that actually need a person.

03

Improve the experience

Faster and more consistent for the customer, not just cheaper for the business — the two usually turn out to be the same thing.

04

Measure the outcome

A system that isn’t measured is just a guess that looks finished. If I can’t point to what changed, I haven’t finished the job.
